## Project Justification

With globalization and the technological revolution, process agility has become essential for success at work. At the same time, interest in a wellness lifestyle continues to grow. In this context, the RAGRO App emerges as a platform that values small-scale farmers, integrates them into modern forms of commerce, and responds to the demand for healthy eating amid fast-paced routines — bridging social awareness and consumer well-being. In this sense, the project promotes not only fair and sustainable trade, but also the rebuilding of community bonds around the production and consumption of food from small producers, modernizing the current processes of selling, logistics, and purchasing, while fostering a sense of community.

---

## Project Objectives

To create a mobile application that directly connects family farming producers with urban consumers, with the goal of promoting smart, sustainable, and modern logistics, with special attention to community engagement. Key features include order scheduling and an intelligent marketplace powered by artificial intelligence, capable of suggesting purchases based on the user's consumption behavior. Other resources include the producer profile — with banking information, the story behind their business, information about production, and a review system — as well as an impact page that presents data on CO₂ reduction in delivery and the number of families benefited by the platform.

---

## High-Level Project Description

### 1. Producer Registration
- Registration of basic producer data (name, CPF/CNPJ, contact, location, type of production, banking details, transport type);
- Simplified food registration in the producer's app;
- Creation of a producer profile with history, photos, description of production, a review system, and a space for sharing the producer's and production's story.

### 2. Production and Logistics Management
- Map integration for geolocation and delivery route optimization;
- Real-time updates of available products;
- Order scheduling with status tracking (production, pickup, delivery);
- Financial dashboard with sales history, revenue, and operational costs.

### 3. Communication and Engagement
- Integrated chat between producer and consumer;
- Engagement forum for the exchange of experiences between producers and consumers.

### 4. Intelligent Marketplace
- Recommendation algorithm for purchases based on consumption behavior;
- Search and filtering system by product type, location, and producer profile;
- Automated suggestions of seasonal foods and sustainable combinations.

### 5. Social and Environmental Impact
- Impact page with CO₂ reduction calculation (delivery);
- Indicators for the number of families supported and volume of sustainable sales.

### 6. Customer Interface
- Registration of basic customer data (name, CPF, contact, location, preferences);
- Food search and purchase.
